Two welcome announcements this week:

  • Apple has just released the XCode UNIX tools, including GCC, as a
    standalone 171MB package for Lion users.     Crucially, it includes the C headers not previously available with Kenneth Reitz's workaround. Which should solve some problems Homebrew had been having.

  • As of Ruby 1.9.3-p125, MRI now has LLVM/Clang compilation support
