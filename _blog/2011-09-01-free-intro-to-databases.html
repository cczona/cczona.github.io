Stanford has an upcoming free online course in databases that they're calling intro-level. But the course description encompasses a lot of good topics beyond SQL that you don't usually get to chew on at that level:

  • XML, DTDs, XML Schema
  • XPath, XQuery, XSLT
  • UML
  • OLAP
  • noSQL
