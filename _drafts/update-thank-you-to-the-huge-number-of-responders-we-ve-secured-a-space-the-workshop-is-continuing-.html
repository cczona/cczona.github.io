UPDATE: Thank you to the huge number of responders. We've secured a space! The workshop is continuing as scheduled.

PREVIOUSLY:
Due to unforeseen circumstances, the location where Railsbridge
Outreach Workshop for Women was going to host this week's workshop has fallen through.

If we can't secure a new location by 3pm today (4/26), we'll have to
cancel the workshop. Not only will this be a bummer for us organizers
who have been toiling to put on the event, it'll really, really suck
for all the folks that have signed up for this event and put effort
into preparing for it.

Therefore, we are reaching out to you to see if your
company can accommodate 60-80 people Friday night and all day
Saturday. If your company in SOMA, Downtown, or Financial District can
accommodate us, please contact me before 3pm today/Thursday.

REQUIREMENTS:

Location - SOMA, Downtown, or Financial district; and walkable from
Muni, CalTrain, and BART.

Room Capacity - Railsbridge will have 40-50 students and 40-45
volunteers this weekend. We need 5 (or more, if available) breakout
rooms for them that are equipped with projector/adaptors, electrical
outlets, and guest wifi access.

Time needed - Friday 5pm -11pm (we'll hopefully be able to leave by
10, but need the buffer); Saturday 8:00am to 5:30pm (possibly as early
as 5pm).

Use of Facilities - Permission to use kitchen snacks/beverages would
be lovely, if the host company is amenable.

Sponsorship of food (nice to have; optional) - Food for Friday (pizza)
and Saturday (lunch, light breakfast). But Railsbridge can cover our
own food costs/arrangements if necessary.

Background info on Railsbridge can be found here:
http://railsbridge.org/
http://www.sfruby.info/events/60258772/

We hope one of you can come through for us. Thank you for your
support of Railsbridge!
