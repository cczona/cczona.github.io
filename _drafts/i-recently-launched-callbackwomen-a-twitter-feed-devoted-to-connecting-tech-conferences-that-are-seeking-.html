I recently launched CallbackWomen, a Twitter feed devoted to connecting tech conferences that are seeking programmers as speakers, with the code women who are speakers are seeking opportunity.

CallbackWomen follows more than 150 conferences, and has already shared a long list of open CFPs. (Many of which are still open right now, by the way...)

What you can do:

* Encourage conference organizers to reach out directly to CallbackWomen, with CFP announcements. Many have found it particularly valuable to explicitly state their commitment to bringing in more proposals from women.

* Follow the CallbackWomen feed to spot new opportunities for you to propose talks. 

* Browse the Follows list to discover interesting conferences that you haven't heard about yet.

* Suggest more conferences for CallbackWomen to Follow. (Curatorial note: limited to conferences which are primarily focused on programming topic and seek programmers as speakers.  The overall universe of tech conferences is too enormous to track.)

* Spread word to peers!
