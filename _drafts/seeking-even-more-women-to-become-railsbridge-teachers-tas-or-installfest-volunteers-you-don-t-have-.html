Seeking: even more women to become Railsbridge teachers, TAs, or installfest volunteers. You don't have to be a "ninja", "guru", "superstar", or "expert". If you've been able to lean across the table and help a classmate or colleague troubleshoot now and then, you've got the chops to be a valuable contributor.

Bonuses: we train you beforehand, the curriculum is easy to present, we teach in teams (no worries about flying solo), we give you tons o' support during events, and you get a free drink at the after-party. Also, participants appreciate the heck out of us. You get to leave with a warm fuzzy glow of having brought excited newbies into the community. Neat!
